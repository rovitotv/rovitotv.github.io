Physics Accurate Layered Sensing Model version 2 for ATR Center

Published: Mon 01 August 2011
Updated: Fri 25 December 2015
By Todd V. Rovito

In Research.

The main focus of this project was to provide an architecture for layered sensing simulation. It is modular in design and constructed on open-source-off- the-shelf-software (Blender, LuxRender, Python, OpenCV, and SUMO).

The project was complied from previous work, fixing many errors such as, multiple vehicle models and color projections. I teamed with and mentored two undergraduate students throughout the process. Their effort enabled them to increase their knowledge and experience with Linux, Python, Ray Tracing, and Geo- Projections.
